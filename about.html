Personal Interests

Besides my interest in computer science, I am also passionate about folk and traditional music and play various instruments like nyckelharpa, guitar, Irish bouzouk and a few more. I have played concerts all over Europe and regularily teach music workshops (for example, I have thaught for Stichting Draailier en Doedelzak, Bordun e.V., Spielkurs Pipenbock and Burg Fürsteneck) and have also organized several concerts and workshops myself. If you're interested in my music, you can take a look at the websites of my bands Fior, Akleja and Airu.
