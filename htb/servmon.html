HTB - {{page.box}} Write-Up

ServMon is a Hack The Box machine and has the IP address 10.10.10.184. It was released on the 11. April 2020, uses a Windows OS and is categorized as an easy machine.

ServMon is a still active machine. The rules of Hack The Box prohibit the publication of write-ups before the boxes are retired. My write-up will appear here once the box has been retired.
